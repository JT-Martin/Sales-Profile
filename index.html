Todd Schuchart

Founder of YouInLocal.com

I worked for Todd for only a few months in 2021 selling his lead generation program for Life Insurance Agents in the US. My responsibilities were mainly to handle sales appointments that were generated for me by the company and then to follow up with anyone who didn't purchase immediately

After a couple months of working for Todd I realized I could generate more appointments for myself by hiring an assistant appointment setter so that is what I did. I spent a couple weeks interviewing and testing different applicants I'd found through Upwork.com and eventually hired someone.

I then spent the next 2 months training her as she learnt to cold call insurance agents and set appointments with them to speak with me.

On average she was generating qualified appointments for me at a total average cost of about $50 USD each.

I was happy with the cost considering she was only working for me part time and I was paying her a premium because of her experience.

If I hired her full-time I think we could have got the cost to around $40/appointment and if I hired someone with less experience and trained them up with the SOP's I built out it could have been more like $25-30/appointment.

Unfortunately after only a short few months of working for Todd he decided to shut the business down to focus on his new business but we parted ways amicably.
